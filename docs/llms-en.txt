# Scrum.org.ua

> A free library of crowdsourced translations of the best Agile, Scrum, and Kanban books and presentations into Ukrainian. Community-driven since 2008.

## Books

- [Scrum and XP from the Trenches](https://scrum.org.ua/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/scrum_xp-from-the-trenches-rus-final.pdf) — Henrik Kniberg, 2008. A practical guide to implementing Scrum and Extreme Programming.
- [Kanban and Scrum: Making the Most of Both](https://scrum.org.ua/wp-content/uploads/ScrumAndKanbanRuFinal.pdf) — Henrik Kniberg & Mattias Skarin, 2010. Comparing Kanban and Scrum, how to combine both approaches.

## About

Scrum.org.ua is a community of enthusiasts that has been crowdsourcing translations of the best books and presentations on agile software development methodologies since 2008. All materials are free and available for download in PDF format.
